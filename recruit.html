Be an AIESECer.

There are two sides to AIESEC. On the face of it, AIESEC provides students with internship positions in 110 countries around the world. The flip side however, is where the real power of AIESEC is revealed. Completely run by students, AIESEC gives you the chance to build your skills, expand your network, and experience the unexpected by putting you in the position of a leader.

The idea behind AIESEC is not simply to provide students with internships abroad. AIESEC is a platform that enables young people to become leaders. We're looking for people who inspire others.
